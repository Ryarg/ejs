Bridie Wareing
Bridie Wareing has worked for 15 years in Higher Education as a Project Manager on numerous initiatives helping individuals to develop their potential. She has initiated and delivered a wider range of training and development solutions to meet the needs of first-time learners, graduates, from management to jobseeks, and SMEs to corporate businesses. Nominated for Lancashire Woman of the year in 2000 for her work with marginal groups in the local community.
 
Tom Rooney
